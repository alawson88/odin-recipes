Cheeseburger

It's burgin' time

Finally. Finally someone understands my love for burgers. You really need to try this thing out! It's burger flippin good!

Ingredients

  • Ketchup
  • Meat
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook meat
  2. Get the ketchup from the fridge door
  3. Cheese slice on top of the meat
  4. Sling some ketchup on that bad boy
  5. Feeling spicy? Add mayo! Wow, that is hot!
  6. Think about it
  7. Buon appetito!
